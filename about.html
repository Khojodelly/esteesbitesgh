Meet The Chef

Passion Behind Every Bite

ESTEESBITES is powered by a chef with a love for preparing tasty meals and creating happy food experiences. The goal is to grow from an online restaurant into a trusted food brand customers can always count on.

Esther Bawanin
Head Chef • ESTEESBITES

Esther Bawanin holds an HND in Hotel Catering and Institution Management from Sunyani Technical University.

She also gained professional experience working for one year at Golden Beam Hotel, Kumasi, where she developed her passion for preparing quality meals and delivering excellent customer satisfaction.
